Top 2: The Anglerfish

The anglerfish are very bony fish. They are named after their charecteristic mode of predation, in which their fin ray acts as a lure for other fish.
Some are pelagic(swimming away from the sea floor), while others are benthic(swimming close to the sea floor).
Angler fishes look prehistoric because they are. These fish were around over 130 million years.
All anglerfish are carnivorous and are adapted for the capture of prey. Their teeth are their most prized possesion which help them with prey grabbing.
