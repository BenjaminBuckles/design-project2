Top 1: The Barreleye fish

The barreleye, also know as the spook fish, is a small deap sea fish found in the tropical waters of the Atlantic, Pacific and Indian Oceans
They are named after their barrel-shaped, tubular eyes, which usually detect prey directly over them.
Suprisingly, they have toothless mouths that are very small. The best part about these fishes is their transparent look, they don't actually have transparent skin just luminous organs.
They swim from 400 to 2500m deep. There fins are spinless and fairly small but their pectoral ones can extend to about half it's body length.
