The Basics
Exotic fish usually are species that are not commonly found in the local or native waters of a particular region. These fish are often looked at for their unique colors, patterns, and behaviors, making them popular choices for aquariums. Some of these fish can be dangerous which is why most of them can't be domestic. Tropical fish are usually praised for their flavor and how versatile they can be as a side dish.

Some interesting facts about exotic fish

  • There are a whoping 1000 different species
  • They all have remarkable eyesight
  • Some can change color which helps them camouflage
  • Some fish use different colors to communicate
